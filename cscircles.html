OUR MISSION

Here at Laurier CS-Circles, we're a group with a purpose!

LARGE PUBLIC Network

Players are allowed to interact with one another and get the skins they want, without having to recieve the same ones from loot boxes.

Featured Skins

Each month we showcase the most traded skin on our featured skin of the month page, and the most popular skins on our trading floor .

SECURE AND SAFE TRANSACTIONS

Our submit a ticket form will allow players to enter all details pertaining to any fraud or scams they may have been a victim of.

CONTINOUS ONLINE SUPPORT

Players can use our contact page to provide us with any questions, comments, or concerns that they may have about the website's current state or any other improvements they would like to see implemented.
